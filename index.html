About Arima Bedside Care

Arima Bedside Care (U) Ltd is a leading healthcare provider in Uganda dedicated to delivering compassionate, professional, and reliable care services to patients in their homes and communities.

Our mission is to bring quality care to you or your loved one, right at the bedside. We understand that health challenges are difficult, which is why we're committed to providing support that extends beyond medical care to encompass emotional and social well-being.

With a team of trained professionals, we serve Kampala and surrounding areas with various healthcare services tailored to meet individual patient needs.
